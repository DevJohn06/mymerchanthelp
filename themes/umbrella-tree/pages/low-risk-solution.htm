What is a Low-Risk Payments Processing Solution?

If you are doing business online, your capability to process client payments is one of the keys to your stability and continuity. However, your ability to do so immediately and safely is fundamental to your success. This is where your payment processors can come to the rescue, hooking you up with an online merchant account. With its help and through a good-old payment gateway, you shall forever lounge in the generosity of your paying customers.

In the world of payment processing, there are low-risk merchants, medium-risk merchants, and high-risk merchants. Risk level is determined on a variety of business factors, such as processing history, country of incorporation, and more.

Merchants classified as “low risk, will typically have all of the following characteristics: low to mid-ticket sales, in an industry with a history of low chargebacks and in an industry that is not politically controversial and is not highly regulated. A brick-and-mortar coffee shop, a bookstore, or a restaurant would be a typical example of a low-risk merchant.
