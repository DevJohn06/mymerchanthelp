Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how National Bookkeeping & Management Systems, Inc. (“NBMS”) uses and protects any information that you provide NBMS.

NBMS is committed to preserving your privacy. If we ask you to provide some data to identify you when you view your account information, you can be assured that this information will only be used in compliance with this Privacy Statement.

NBMS has a firm policy to protect the confidentiality and security of our customers' information. We do not share your non‐public personal information with unaffiliated third parties. Data will only be exchanged with your permission in compliance with all applicable laws, except for the specific purposes below. Please read this policy carefully. It gives you important information about how we handle your personal information.

NBMS reserves the right to change this policy at any time.

Use of information

We restrict the collection and use of non-public personal information to the minimum that we think is necessary to provide you with superior service. Services can include advising you about our products, services and other opportunities, maintaining your account with us and administering our business.

What we Collect

We obtain most non‐public personal information directly from you or your agents whether in person, by telephone or electronically.

We may obtain the following information:

  • First name, last name and job title
  • Contact information including home address, email address, business address, home telephone numbers and business telephone numbers
  • Social Security Numbers
  • Employee identification Numbers
  • Banking information such as as wiring information
  • Accounts with us and transaction by us, with us or through us

Disclosure

We do not disclose any kind of non-public personal information about our customers or former customers to anyone except when we believe it is necessary for the conduct of our business or when it is required by law to disclose it. Without your consent, we will not make any disclosures of nonpublic personal information to other companies who may want to sell their products or services to you, except in those specific, limited situations.

What we do with the information we gather

We require this information in order to understand your needs and provide better service, particularly for the following reasons:

  • Internal record keeping
  • We send email about new products, services, or other information which we think may be valuable using the email address which you have provided
  • From time to time, we may also use your information to contact you for general account information. We may contact you by email, phone, fax or mail
  • We may use the information to customize our website to provide better service to you
  • We are required by law to provide certain information, including social security numbers and addresses, to the Internal Revenue Service and state and local taxing authorities

Controlling your personal information

Unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so, we will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties.
